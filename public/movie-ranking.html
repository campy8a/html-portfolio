The Best Movies according to Felipe

My top 3 movies of all time

Openheimer

The history of the atomic bomb, showing all of its process. Almost shows you the excitement and fear of its creators.

Inception

A journey to the subconscious of the mind, another Nolan movie that keeps you on the edge and thinking long after leaving the cinema

The Lion King (1994)

The best representation of Hamlet for the kids, thanks to disney with memorable songs and moments we remember 'till today
